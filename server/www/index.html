GeoQuery.in

GeoQuery.in is set to become India's leading free and open source mapping tool, dedicated to offering precise geographical information accessible to everyone. Our mission is to democratize geographical data access, empowering individuals, businesses, and researchers with the tools they need to make well-informed decisions.

Openness

Upholding open-source principles to ensure transparency and encourage community-driven enhancements. Open data as well as implementation.

Inclusivity

Ensuring easy access to geographical data for all users, regardless of their technical background or expertise.

Innovation

Continually incorporating advanced technology for enhanced data representation and analytical capabilities.

Collaboration

Building strong partnerships with government entities, NGOs, educational institutions, and the global open-source community.
